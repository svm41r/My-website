FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

What file formats do you accept for raw footage?
I accept all major formats — MP4, MOV, MKV, ProRes, and more. If you're shooting on phone or camera, just send what you have and I'll handle the rest. Cloud links (Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer) all work perfectly.
How long does a typical edit take?
Starter edits are delivered within 5 business days, and Pro edits within 3 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee if you need it faster.
Do you write scripts or just edit?
Both! I can work with your existing script or help structure one from scratch. For faceless content especially, I'm happy to assist with hook writing and narrative flow before the edit begins.
Can I request specific music or sound effects?
Absolutely. If you have a specific track in mind, send it over. Otherwise, I source royalty-free music from premium libraries that fits the exact mood and energy of your content.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
That's what revisions are for. Starter packages include 2 rounds of revisions, and Pro packages include unlimited revisions. I work until you're genuinely happy with the final product.
Do you offer bulk deals for ongoing content?
Yes! If you're producing content regularly, I offer monthly packages at significantly reduced rates. Reach out through the contact form and we can build a custom plan that fits your volume.
